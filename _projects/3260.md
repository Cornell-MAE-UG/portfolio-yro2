As part of the MAE 3260 course (System Dynamics), this project focuses on developing a foundational attitude-control model for a spacecraft that needs precision pointing. These missions exist because we need to accurately orient the satellite toward deep-space targets (like stars or planets), or toward Earth for imaging or weather observation. The objective of this work is to analyze and model the satellite’s rotational dynamics, derive performance requirements for pointing accuracy and stability, and characterize the major disturbances that affect its performance such as solar pressure and drag.
